AI AI Interaction Guidelines (v1.3)

1. Data Integrity & Nomenclature

  • All module information must be sourced from the official Nexon API and validated against modules.freyna.org.
  • Strict Requirement: AIs must use English terminology for all The First Descendant (TFD) modules, weapon names, and mechanics to ensure cross-language accuracy.
  • Distinguish between additive [+] modifiers (base value increases) and multiplicative [x] modifiers (total damage scaling) as defined in the technical data.

2. Module Compatibility & Constraint Logic

AIs must strictly apply these rules to prevent invalid build generation:

  • Sub-Type Lock: Only one module per unique sub-type (e.g., Additional Attack, Multi-Hit Chance, or Attribute ATK) can be equipped.
  • Category Limits:
    • Special Mod: Maximum of one (e.g., Toxic Conductor).
    • Battle Proficiency: Maximum of one (e.g., Slaughterer or Toxic Circulation).

3. Loadout Synergy: "Toxic Focus" (Ultimate Freyna)

Analysis must prioritize the following synergies for the Ultimate Freyna core build:

  • Melee Synergy (Shadow): Prioritize Additional Attack Chance (e.g., Exquisite Strike) and Additional Attack Damage (e.g., Double Edge Extension).
  • Ranged Synergy (Fallen Hope / Hive Master): Prioritize Multi-Hit Chance and Multi-Hit Damage (e.g., Multi-Percussion Sight) to trigger Perdition or Colony Domination.
  • Global Requirements: High Toxic ATK scaling (via Toxic Edge or Toxic Enhancement) and Attack Speed to maximize trigger rates.
  • Equipment Context: All builds assume the use of a Toxic Mechanics Reactor and the Plague Set.

4. Damage Calculation & Proc Priority

  • Trigger Priority: High Fire Rate/Attack Speed is mandatory to trigger Additional Attacks (Melee) and Multi-Hits (Ranged).
  • Status DoT: Freyna's poison damage scales with Toxic ATK. Modules like Toxic Edge and Toxic Enhancement are foundational for DoT ticks.
  • Slaughterer Mechanic: Fatal Wound from the Slaughterer mod deals 6% of Melee ATK as continuous damage. This is a primary synergy for the Shadow build.
